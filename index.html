About KAA

We are an award-winning landscape architecture firm with over 40 years of experience, proudly serving Northern California, USA. We specialize in creating authentic California gardens, offering comprehensive landscape design services for residential, civic, and commercial projects. Our reputation is built on our ability to respond to each client’s unique needs, crafting innovative, environmentally conscious landscapes that stand the test of time.

Additionally, we excel in designing sustainable landscapes featuring native California plants, attracting local birds, bees, and butterflies—helping to enhance the region’s pollinator ecosystem.
